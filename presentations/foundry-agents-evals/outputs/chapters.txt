This session is the third and final part of the "Host your Python agents on Microsoft Foundry" series, covering quality evaluations and safety evaluations for hosted agents. It demonstrates built-in evaluators, batch/scheduled/continuous evaluations, evaluation alerts, automated red teaming, guardrails, and content filter error handling.
